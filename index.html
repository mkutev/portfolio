Hi :)

My name is Momchil Kutev and I'm a front-end developer and UI designer, graduated from SFU's Interactive Arts & Technology program in Vancouver, Canada.

Through the perspective of human-centered design, I love creating well-thought-out and enjoyable digital experiences by considering the user at every step of the development process. I love starting with thorough research and simple sketches and iterating towards a great product or service, and my background in graphic design and front-end development helps greatly in finding a suitable balance between form and function for each project. I value taking risks, limiting scope, and being able to communicate a guiding vision. I also like cars, Counter-Strike, and sketching with my eyes closed.

After just completing my undergraduate degree, I’m currently pursuing Web and Interface Design opportunities, both full and part time.
